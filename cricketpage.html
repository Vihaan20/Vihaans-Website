this is about one of my hobbies, cricket

Cricket is a ball and bat game where the first batting team tries to hit the ball to get runs and the bowling team tries to hit the stumps behind the batter to get him out of the game

rules

batting side

for batting if you hit the ball to the end of the boundry it is a four but if you hit it and it stays in the air until the boundry its a 6

after the bowler bowls the ball you are able to run between stumps to get extra runs if you haven't scored a 6/4

bowling side

for bowling you have to bowl the ball like this, which is slightly different from just throwing the ball.

When you bowl, if you bowl out of a certain range it is considered a no ball since it will either be very hard or impossible for the batter to hit the ball
